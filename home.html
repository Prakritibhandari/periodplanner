Menstruation Cycle

Menstruation is a natural monthly process where the body sheds the lining of the uterus.
It usually lasts 3 to 7 days and is a sign of a healthy reproductive system. While it can
bring physical and emotional changes, understanding your menstrual
cycle helps you care for your body better and live in sync with your energy levels.

Phases of Menstruation

1. Menstrual Phase (Period)
Days 1 to 5

Energy: Low, feel tired or slow
Feelings: Sensitive, introspective, calm or sometimes irritable

Tasks to prioritize:
- Rest and self-care
- Light, creative work or planning
- Avoid big decisions or high-energy tasks

2. Follicular Phase
Days 1 to 13

Energy: Rising, more focused and motivated
Feelings: Optimistic, curious, energized

Tasks to prioritize:
- Learning new skills
- Planning and brainstorming
- Problem-solving and creative work

3. Ovulation
Around Day 14

Energy: Peak energy, confidence, social mood
Feelings: Outgoing, communicative, enthusiastic

Tasks to prioritize:
- Collaboration and networking
- Important meetings or presentations
- Creative and social projects

4. Luteal Phase
Days 15 to 28

Energy: High at first, then dips
Feelings: Productive early; later PMS symptoms

Tasks to prioritize:
- Completing and organizing work
- Detail-focused tasks
- Rest and emotional self-care toward end
